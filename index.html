About

!Eagle Ridge Iris Garden came to fruition in 1998. It all began with owner-operator, Delane, sitting on the curb watching his grandmother tend her iris garden, and later his love of photographing wild flowers blossomed into a new passion. Located, in the Emerald Hills of Billings, MT, over 3000 varieties of iris from all different classes are grown. The bloom generally begins mid April and lasts though the 4th of July with some re-bloomers in the Fall. Lovingly tended by a Great Dane, an old pug, a white cat, Delane and Jane the iris are flourishing beyond their wildest dreams!
