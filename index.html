About

Eagle Ridge Iris Gardens came to fruition in 1998. It all began with owner-operator, Delane, sitting on the curb watching his grandmother tend her iris garden, and later his love of photographing wild flowers blossomed into a new passion. Located in the beautiful Emerald Hills of Billings, MT, over 3000 varieties of iris from all different classes are grown. The bloom generally begins mid-April and lasts though the 4th of July. Lovingly tended by Iris the pug, Brian the Great Dane, George the cat, Delane and Jane, the iris are flourishing beyond their wildest dreams!
